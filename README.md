# DeepShield
### AMD-Powered Portable Deepfake Video Detection Device

---

## About
DeepShield is a portable USB device powered by AMD's Ryzen AI chip 
that detects deepfake videos completely offline, on any device, 
without internet connection.

---

## Problem
- Deepfake videos are being used for fraud, blackmail and misinformation
- Existing tools require uploading videos to cloud servers — privacy risk
- No offline, portable solution exists for common users
- Non-technical users have no simple way to verify video authenticity

---

## Our Solution
Plug DeepShield into any laptop, select video, 
get instant REAL or FAKE result in seconds.

No internet. No cloud. No technical knowledge needed.
Your video never leaves your device.

---

## How It Works
1. Plug DeepShield USB into any laptop or PC
2. Select suspicious video file
3. AMD AI chip processes video locally
4. Get instant result — REAL or DEEPFAKE
5. Confidence score displayed with flagged frames
